Flash on a tube!? Blasphemy!

This page contains a simple flash element (simple in that it's only text and images). Flash elements are animatinos/games/guis/html objects that a creative and VERY patient individual can embed in a website. Flash actually runs out of a script (Actionscript) - that allows someone like me to create something fancy.

This flash animation uses a few simple tweens and some masks (actually, a whole bunch of masks) to hide and reveal moving elements sort of in time to the music.
