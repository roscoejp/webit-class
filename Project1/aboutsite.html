About the site.

This site is hosted on the UCF Eustis server.
Thanks UCF for such a fast server.

I'm hoping the site gives off a professional feel, I was inspired by a few other websites I visit off and on and the layout turned out pretty good. I also want to mention that my site uses XHTML Strict 1.0 and validated the first time I put it through the checker... no lie.

It took me about 2 hours to both prototype and code the header and the top half of the main css sheet.

The biggest part of the site would have to be the header (including the nav bar). It's the most notable part of the site, and the hardest to develope.

The header uses a wrapper div to hold some more divs that contain all of those pretty links you see up there. The navbar is also up there (and the sub-navbar is inside of THAT wrapper). All in all, there are about 7 divs holding together the top of the page.

ON TOP OF THAT, I have another container class conveniently called "container" holding the entire page together and centering it on the screen (to a minimum of 900 pixels). So, as long as your screen is bigger than 12", the whole site'll be centered right in the middle of your screen!
This site was developed using Google Chrome, Notepad++, and both Adobe Flash and Adobe Photoshop. I also have to give credit to "The Consumerist" and "BBC", both of whom have given me inspiration for my layout and design of this website. I also want to say, if you're still using Firefox or Internet Explorer, you're missing out. Google Chrome is wonderful. And it's faster than a Potato. More on that here.
All pages on this site are valid under XHTML Strict 1.0. All CSS documents are also valid under W3C's CSS validation.
